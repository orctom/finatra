Finatra 1.6.0 Released

posted by capotej on 2015-01-09 03:00:00 -0800

Last release for 1.x.x and Scala 2.9

As we prepare for the upcoming 2.x.x release, this will be the last 1.x.x release and also the last release to be published for scala 2.9.x.

New Finatra and Maintainer!

I’m happy to announce that Steve Cosenza will be taking over the project for the 2.x.x release and beyond. We’re excited to release the next major iteration of Finatra and share everything we’ve learned using it in production at Twitter over the course of a year.
